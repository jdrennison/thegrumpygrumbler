"When was the last time you saw Mr. David Anderson?" asked the Met detective.
"Well, he came to the office two days ago, went on his lunch break and never came back." said the director.
"His wife called the office the next day and when we told her he's not come back since last afternoon she said she would call the police"
"I see. What is the position held by Mr. Anderson in your company?"
"He is an IT project manager. He manages the timescales and the delivery of our technology projects".
"That's all I needed to ask. If anything turns up will you call me on this number please."
"Of course. Oh there's something else which I don't know if it matters much but I think I should tell you anyway. The last thing he said before he disappeared was to one of the guys in the development team. I overheard him because I happened to be walking by at the time."
"He said. 'It can't be a big job can it? We are already doing this in another part of the codebase'."
