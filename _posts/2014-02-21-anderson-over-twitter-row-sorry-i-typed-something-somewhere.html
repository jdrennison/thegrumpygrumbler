A Starfleet officer who yesterday came under fire for referring to a sentient android as "it" on Twitter, has issued a public apology.

Commander (Op-Spec) David Anderson said he should have "thought twice" before posting his tweet.

"I generally censor myself before I tweet something but it seems that people these days get self righteous over the most insignificant shit."

"Will a grovelling apology save me from a dishonourable discharge and my name from being smeared more than it already is? If so, I'm sorry alright? I'm sorry I posted this on the internet."
