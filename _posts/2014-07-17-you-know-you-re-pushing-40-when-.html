Forty isn't a good age to be. To prove the point, find me just one person who has said once in their lifetime "God, I wish I could accelerate time and turn forty right now. The wait is killing me, pretty please, I want to be forty!"

Granted we didn't do that when we were approaching 30 but it was a lot more bearable in that you were a full-grown adult, had had about a decade of independence from home and you were one more decade away from being middle-aged. For women, they have ten more years to bear children.

When you finally do turn 40 you realise that rather than suddenly becoming 40, you've been becoming 40 since you realised that certain things have been happening.

Women that look roughly ten years younger than you want children.
Men your age have gone through a divorce.
Friends disappear because they have two toddlers to begin with.
