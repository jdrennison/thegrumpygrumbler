Iceland. It's far away, it's small we don't hear about it often. In my mind, it was a place to which I hadn't given much thought but I had it at the back of my mind as a weekend destination. In short, it was up there and we left it alone and it did likewise.

That was before the volcano. I suppose you can see where I'm going with this. I thought volcanoes existed only in fiction. I had seen one or two in the Smurfs back in the 80s, I've also seen "Volcano" and "Dante's Peak". And I thought that was the end of it. Alright, volcanoes exist but it's not every time one erupts that wrecks a continent's airspace.

I haven't written anything here for a month and it would probably be two months if volcanoes didn't just decide to blow up just three days before my holiday abroad.

Fucking volcano, man. Can you fucking believe it.
